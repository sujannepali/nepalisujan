About Me

Geomatics Engineer | Geospatial enthusiast | GIS & Remote Sensing Expert

With over 6 years of hands-on experience in geospatial technologies, I bring a unique blend of technical precision and academic depth to every project. Currently, I serve as a geospatial expert at ICIMOD, where I deliver high-impact solutions in renewable energy, for the identification of energy demand in the potential area for river based lift irrigation using advanced GIS and remote sensing tools and data analysis. The platform for this solutions is prepared for Nepal and Bhutan and we called it PURE platform.

My journey for 4 years was in academia, where I dedicated as a Geomatics instructor in GIS, Remote Sensing, Cartography, and Surveying. This teaching experience not only deepened my technical foundation but also sharpened my ability to translate complex concepts into actionable insights, a skill I carry into every aspect of my work.

Whether building platforms, analyzing satellite imagery, or mentoring the next generation of geomatics professionals, I thrive on transforming geospatial data into real-world impact. I dont just map the world, I help shape smarter, more sustainable decisions through spatial intelligence.
